<p><p>The manual door closer market encompasses various types designed for different installation needs and aesthetics. Surface applied door closers are mounted on the door's surface, offering easy installation and visibility. Concealed door closers are hidden within the door frame, providing a sleek appearance suitable for upscale settings. Floor springs are installed within the floor to control door movement and are ideal for heavy doors in commercial spaces. Each type serves distinct functionality while enhancing door operation and building aesthetics.</p></p>
