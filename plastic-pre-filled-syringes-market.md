<p><p>The Plastic Pre-Filled Syringes market is categorized into several types based on the polymer materials used. COP (cyclic olefin polymer) offers excellent barrier properties and thermal stability, making it suitable for sensitive biologics. COC (cyclic olefin copolymer) provides superior clarity and lower moisture permeability, enhancing visibility and stability. PP (polypropylene) is cost-effective and widely used, with strong chemical resistance. Other materials may include styrenics and polycarbonate, each contributing unique advantages for specific pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
