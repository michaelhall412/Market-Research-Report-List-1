<p><p>The Digital-to-Analog Converter (DAC) market encompasses various types, including R-2R, high-speed current-steering, and others. R-2R DACs utilize resistors in a specific configuration to convert digital signals to analog, known for efficiency and simplicity. High-speed current-steering DACs offer rapid conversion by switching currents, ideal for high-frequency applications. Other types include sigma-delta and pulse-width modulation DACs, which serve diverse needs across industries like audio processing, telecommunications, and embedded systems, ensuring accurate and high-quality signal conversions.</p></p>
