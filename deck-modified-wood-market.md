<p><p>The Deck Modified Wood market comprises three main types: Thermal Treatment, Acetylation, and Other Treatments. Thermal Treatment enhances wood's durability and resistance to moisture through high-temperature processing, improving its stability and lifespan. Acetylation involves chemically altering wood fibers, reducing moisture absorption and enhancing dimensional stability. Other Treatments may include various chemical processes or coatings to increase resistance to pests, decay, and weathering. Together, these methods improve the performance and longevity of wood used in outdoor decking applications.</p></p>
