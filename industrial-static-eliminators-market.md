<p><p>Industrial static eliminators can be categorized into several types based on their design and application. Bar type eliminators consist of elongated bars that emit ionized air to neutralize static charges over a wide area. Nozzle type devices focus ionized air in a concentrated stream, making them suitable for targeted applications. Fan type eliminators utilize fans to disperse ionized air across larger surfaces, while gun type units offer handheld convenience for spot treatment. Other types may include innovative or specialized designs catering to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
