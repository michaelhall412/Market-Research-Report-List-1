<p><p>Industrial Remote Terminal Units (RTUs) are critical components in monitoring and controlling industrial operations. The market is segmented into two main types: Wireless Industrial RTUs and Wired Industrial RTUs. </p><p>Wireless Industrial RTUs utilize radio, cellular, or satellite communication, offering flexibility in deployment and reducing installation costs. They are ideal for remote or difficult-to-access locations. Conversely, Wired Industrial RTUs rely on physical cabling for communication, providing stable and secure connections, making them suitable for environments requiring high reliability and data integrity.</p></p>
