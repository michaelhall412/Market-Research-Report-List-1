<p><ul><li>Brightfield</li><li>Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The virtual microscopy market is divided into two primary types: brightfield and fluorescence microscopy. Brightfield microscopy involves illuminating a specimen with visible light, allowing for detailed imaging of stained biological samples. It is widely used in pathology and education. Fluorescence microscopy, on the other hand, utilizes fluorescent dyes to visualize specific cellular components, enabling the observation of structures and processes that are often invisible under brightfield conditions. These technologies enhance diagnostic accuracy and research capabilities in various fields.</p></p>
