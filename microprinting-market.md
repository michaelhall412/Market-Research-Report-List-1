<p><p>The microprinting market encompasses various specialized technologies. UV invisible marking utilizes ultraviolet ink that is visible only under UV light, enhancing security. Special inks include a range of unique formulations for specific applications, offering customization. Infrared ink marking employs inks detectable by infrared sensors, ensuring secrecy. Magnetic ink features additives that magnetize, suitable for checks and financial documents. Micro-embossing creates raised patterns for tamper resistance. Other methods may involve advanced techniques like digital printing or holography, broadening the market's scope.</p></p>
