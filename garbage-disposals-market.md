<p><p>The garbage disposal market primarily consists of two types: batch feed and continuous feed systems. Batch feed disposals operate by processing food waste in specific batches, requiring users to cover the unit with a lid before activation, enhancing safety. Continuous feed disposals, on the other hand, allow for continuous operation while running water, making them more convenient for ongoing food waste disposal. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with batch feed often favored for safety and continuous feed for efficiency.</p></p>
