<p><p>The mammography detectors market comprises three main types: Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors, Flat Panel Detectors, and CMOS Flat Detectors. CR Detectors use imaging plates to capture and convert X-rays into digital signals, offering flexibility and lower costs. Flat Panel Detectors provide high-resolution images and faster processing times, enhancing diagnostic accuracy. CMOS Flat Detectors employ complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, delivering compact designs with improved image quality and lower radiation doses. Together, these technologies drive advancements in breast cancer screening and diagnostics.</p></p>
