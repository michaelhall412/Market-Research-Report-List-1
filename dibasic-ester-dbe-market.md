<p><p>Dibasic esters (DBE) are versatile solvents and chemical intermediates widely used in coatings, adhesives, and plastics. The market comprises various types, including Dimethyl Adipate (DMA), Dimethyl Glutarate (DMG), Dimethyl Succinate (DMS), and mixed DBE. DMA is known for its low toxicity and high solvency properties, while DMG and DMS offer excellent performance in specific applications. Mixed DBE combines different esters to provide tailored properties, enhancing their utility across diverse industrial sectors, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer goods.</p></p>
