<p><p>The Automotive Torque Vectoring System market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I systems distribute torque between the front and rear wheels, enhancing stability and traction during acceleration and cornering. Type II systems further refine this by distributing torque between individual wheels on the same axle, allowing for improved handling and agility. These systems are increasingly adopted in performance vehicles and luxury cars, driving their growth in the automotive industry.</p></p>
