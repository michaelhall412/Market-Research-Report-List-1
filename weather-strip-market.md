<p><p>The weather strip market comprises various materials, primarily EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer), and TPO (Thermoplastic Olefin). EPDM offers excellent weather resistance and durability, ideal for outdoor applications. TPE provides flexibility and recyclability, making it popular in consumer products. TPO combines the properties of plastic and rubber, delivering strength and UV resistance, suitable for automotive applications. Other materials in the market include PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) and silicone, each catering to specific performance and environmental needs.</p></p>
