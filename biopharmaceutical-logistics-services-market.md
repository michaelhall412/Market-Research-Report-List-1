<p><p>Biopharmaceutical logistics services encompass two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics is essential for maintaining temperature-sensitive biopharmaceuticals, ensuring safe transport from production through delivery. This involves specialized packaging, refrigerated vehicles, and real-time monitoring of temperature conditions. Non-cold chain logistics, on the other hand, pertains to biopharmaceutical products that can be stored and transported at ambient temperatures. Both segments are crucial for the efficient distribution of biopharmaceuticals, catering to varying product stability requirements.</p></p>
