<p><p>The waste oil recycling market encompasses various types of oils, each with distinct applications. Fuel oil, derived from recycled products, serves as an alternative energy source. Lubricating oil is processed to restore viscosity and performance for automotive and industrial machinery. Hydraulic oil is reclaimed for use in equipment that relies on hydraulic systems. Transformer oil is recycled for electrical insulation and cooling. Cooking oil, typically from food establishments, is repurposed for biodiesel production. Other oils may include specialty or niche applications, contributing to sustainability efforts.</p></p>
