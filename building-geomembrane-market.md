<p><strong>The Building Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Rough</li><li>Double Rough</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building geomembrane market is categorized into single rough and double rough types. Single rough geomembranes feature one textured side, enhancing friction and anchorage while providing effective fluid containment. In contrast, double rough geomembranes have textured surfaces on both sides, further increasing friction and aiding in soil stabilization, making them ideal for applications requiring higher slip resistance. Both types serve critical roles in environmental protection, waste containment, and construction, offering varying levels of performance depending on project requirements.</p></p>
