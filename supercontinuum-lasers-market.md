<p><p>Supercontinuum lasers are categorized into two main market types based on their wavelength capabilities: MIR (Mid-Infrared) lasers and Visible/NIR (Near-Infrared) lasers. MIR lasers are designed for applications like chemical sensing and environmental monitoring, offering wavelengths that penetrate materials for detailed analysis. In contrast, Visible/NIR lasers provide broader spectra useful in fields like spectroscopy, medical diagnostics, and optical coherence tomography. Both types enable advancements in research and industrial applications through their unique wavelength characteristics and broad spectral output.</p></p>
