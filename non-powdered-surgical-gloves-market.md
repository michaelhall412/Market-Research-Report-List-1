<p><p>The non-powdered surgical gloves market consists of various types, including Natural Rubber Latex (NRL), Synthetics, Polyisoprene (PI), Neoprene, and Nitrile. NRL gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort but can cause allergies. Synthetics provide alternative options that are latex-free. Polyisoprene gloves mimic latex's properties while being hypoallergenic. Neoprene gloves offer chemical resistance and durability. Nitrile gloves are known for their puncture resistance and are widely used in high-risk situations, making them a popular choice in healthcare settings.</p></p>
