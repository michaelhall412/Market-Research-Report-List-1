<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Light Imager Market primarily comprises two types of imaging technologies: CCD (Charge Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are renowned for their superior image quality, low noise, and uniformity, making them ideal for professional photography and scientific applications, especially in low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS sensors have gained popularity due to their lower power consumption, compact design, and faster processing speeds, making them suitable for consumer electronics, surveillance systems, and mobile devices. Each technology caters to different market needs and preferences.</p></p>
