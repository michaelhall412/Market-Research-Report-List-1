<p><p>The air humidifiers market includes various types, primarily vapor type and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers use heat to produce steam, releasing warm moisture into the air, ideal for dry environments and enhancing comfort. In contrast, water spray humidifiers utilize a fan to disperse tiny water droplets, effectively increasing humidity levels without heating the air. Both types play a crucial role in improving indoor air quality, preventing dryness-related health issues, and promoting overall well-being.</p></p>
