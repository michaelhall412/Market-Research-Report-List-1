<p><p>Gas outlets are categorized into three main types based on their installation and use: wall outlets, console outlets, and ceiling outlets. Wall outlets are typically mounted on vertical surfaces, providing easy access for gas appliances. Console outlets are often freestanding units, designed for specific appliances like heaters. Ceiling outlets are installed overhead, commonly used for suspended gas heating systems. Each type serves distinct purposes and applications, catering to diverse needs in residential and commercial environments while ensuring safe gas distribution.</p></p>
