<p><p>The cardiovascular catheters market is divided into two primary types: therapeutic and diagnostic catheters. Cardiovascular therapeutic catheters are designed for treatment purposes, including procedures like angioplasty, stenting, and delivering medications directly to the heart. In contrast, cardiovascular diagnostic catheters are used for examination and assessment, enabling healthcare providers to visualize blood flow and heart functions through procedures such as pressure measurement and contrast imaging. Both types are essential for effective cardiovascular disease management and intervention.</p></p>
