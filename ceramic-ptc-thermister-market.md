<p><p>The ceramic PTC thermistor market is categorized into two main types: surface mount type and radial leaded type. Surface mount thermistors are compact and designed for direct mounting on circuit boards, offering space efficiency and reliability in modern electronics. Radial leaded thermistors, on the other hand, feature leads extending radially, making them suitable for through-hole applications. Both types are utilized in various industries, including automotive and consumer electronics, for temperature sensing and overcurrent protection, catering to diverse design requirements.</p></p>
