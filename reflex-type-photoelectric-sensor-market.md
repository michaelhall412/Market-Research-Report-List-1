<p><strong>The Reflex Type Photoelectric Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Retroreflective Photoelectric Sensor</li><li>Diffuse Reflective Photoelectric Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflex type photoelectric sensor market primarily includes retroreflective and diffuse reflective sensors. Retroreflective photoelectric sensors utilize a reflector to bounce the emitted light back to the sensor, allowing for long detection ranges and reliable operation even in challenging environments. In contrast, diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors detect objects based on the light reflected directly from the target back to the sensor. This type is ideal for close-range applications and offers simpler installations, making both types essential for industrial automation and safety applications.</p></p>
