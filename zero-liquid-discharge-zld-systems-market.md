<p><p>Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) systems aim to minimize liquid waste by recovering and reusing water, while eliminating discharge. The conventional ZLD market consists of traditional technologies like evaporation and crystallization, effective for high-salinity wastewater. In contrast, the hybrid ZLD market combines multiple methods, such as membrane filtration and advanced evaporation techniques. This approach enhances efficiency and reduces operational costs by utilizing the strengths of different technologies, making it suitable for varying industrial applications and increasingly stringent regulations on wastewater discharge.</p></p>
