<p><strong>The Gas Turbine Electrical Power Generation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT)</li><li>Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Gas Turbine Electrical Power Generation market comprises two primary types: Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT) and Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT). OCGT systems operate by directly converting gas combustion into electricity, allowing for quick startup and flexibility, making them ideal for peak load demand. Conversely, CCGT systems utilize a two-step process where exhaust heat from the gas turbine is used to generate steam, increasing overall efficiency and output, thus catering to baseload and mid-level demands in power generation.</p></p>
