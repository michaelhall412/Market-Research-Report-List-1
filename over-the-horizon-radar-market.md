<p><p>The Over-the-horizon Radar market is primarily categorized into two types: Skywave and Groundwave. </p><p>Skywave Over-the-horizon Radar utilizes high-frequency radio waves that bounce off the ionosphere, allowing long-distance detection. This method is effective for tracking airborne and maritime targets. </p><p>In contrast, Groundwave Over-the-horizon Radar relies on radio waves that follow the Earth's surface, making it suitable for short-range surveillance and target identification. Both types serve critical roles in military and civilian applications, enhancing situational awareness and security.</p></p>
